Wooden City Tacoma

Published at 10 April 2022

Great drinks, comforting American Cuisine & sincere hospitality in the heart of downtown Tacoma!

Perfectly captures the quintessential pacific northwest modern American dining experience. Rustic building features combined with contemporary touches throughout. Seating space is "intimate", not at all unique for a restaurant with this level popularity or aesthetic. That aside we thoroughly enjoyed all of two hours here; taking full advantage of the menu from appetizers to dessert. We loved every bite. Oh,and do NOT skip out dessert. Only a couple offerings but they alone are enough to induce coming in as part of an evening on the town. Pricing is on par with that of similar restaurants in the Seattle area (my opinion). Our server was incredibly personable and knowledgeable. Never acted rushed or impatient even though they were near capacity. This restaurant is a great addition to Tacoma and is helping to bring a hip trendy food vibe into the downtown Tacoma area.

My favorite dish are Blisterd Hungarian Peppers, Smoked Salmon Toast and Lamb Bologness! Must try! I really recommend this place.
